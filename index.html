Evony Salmeron

Evony "Evon" Salmeron is a senior journalism major at the University of Maryland with a passion for pop culture reporting and social video creation

She has contributed to a number of on-campus publicatons as well as reporting on a variety of beats, including fashion, pop culture, and general student life.

Currently she is a part of the Audience Engagement Bureau for Capital News Service Maryland as an audience engagement editor. There she showcases her ability to analyze audience behavior, engagement metrics, and feedback to understand what types of content perform best. She uses this data to guide future content decisions and improve audience interactions all with the goal of guiding audiences to the publication's site.
